About Me

I'm a seasoned technical leader of early- and growth-stage startups, often serving as the first hire focused on engineering leadership and people development. I've had the privilege of building high-performing teams working on highly-technical products, including JazzHR, Deis/Helm (now part of Microsoft Azure), and Algorithmia.

In my career, I've discovered that my personal mission is to foster others' professional and personal flourishing. Leveraging my experience building and leading high-performing technical teams at early-stage startups, I partner with technical leaders (typically CTOs and VPs of Engineering) to help them think about technical architecture, technical process, and engineering organizational growth (recruiting, culture and team building, mentoring first-time managers, etc.). I offer one-on-one coaching with leaders as well as whole-team mentoring and workshops.
