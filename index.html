About Me

I'm a seasoned technical leader of early- and growth-stage startups, often as the first hire focused on engineering leadership and people development. I've had the privilege of building high-performing teams to work on highly-technical products, including JazzHR, Deis/Helm (now part of Microsoft Azure), and Algorithmia.

My passion is fostering the personal growth and professional development of others, so they can flourish personally and professionally. Leveraging my experience building and leading high-performing teams at early-stage startups, I partner with founders, technical executives, and leaders to help them think about technical architecture, product process, and organizational growth. I offer one-on-one coaching with leaders as well as whole-team mentoring and workshops.

I'd love to work with your team!
